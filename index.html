Intersecting Hands




Our Mission

Intersecting Hands is a non-profit organization with the purpose of supporting women of color and spreading awareness on the issues women of color face. Our organization gives women a platform to speak on their issues and get support from a community that understands them and has similar experiences.

Including Women of Color in the Feminist Narrative

Although this non-profit was founded for women of color, we understand there are many different kinds of intersectional feminism and are always welcome to any women. We work to make feminism a more inclusive movement, for all women and bring light to issues that marginalized groups of women face.
