About Us



Feminism has a million different definitions depending on who you may ask. To a white woman it may look entirely different than to a woman of color. Here at ourorganization we strive to promote intersectionality within feminism so that women of color are always included in the conversation. The struggles women of color face are often different than the ones an average white woman may face. We seek to educate to spread awareness and offer a safe space and platform for women of color to speak on their struggles.
